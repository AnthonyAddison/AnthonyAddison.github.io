3. Strategies for Generating Traffic and Income#

It is important to identify a suitable niche for your blog. Desirable topics include current affairs, financial advice, lifestyle, technology, travel, beauty, and fashion. Another key element for making money with your blog is to decide on the monetization strategies. This can include affiliate marketing, sponsored content, digital products, and more.
