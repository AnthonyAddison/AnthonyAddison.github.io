III. Requirements for Investing in Indian Stock Markets#

To begin investing in the Indian stock market, you need an account with a stockbroker or a trading platform. To open an account, you will need a valid PAN card and proof of identity, such as your driver’s license or passport. You will also need to provide proof of address, such as a utility bill or bank statement. Additionally, you will need a registered bank account to facilitate transactions to and from your stockbroking account.

The next step is to decide which app best suits your needs. There are various app offerings that specialize in different investment strategies. You may choose an app based on its fees, security features, user reviews or platform features. Do your research beforehand to make sure that the app you select is credible, secure and trusted, and aligned with your investment objectives.
